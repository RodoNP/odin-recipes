Arroz con leche

Arroz con leche (rice pudding) is a famous mexican dessert wich consists mainly of rice mixed with water and/or milk and cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • Water: cook the rice in two cups of water
  • Cinnamon: cook the rice with cinnamon sticks. Plus, use ground cinnamon to garnish.
  • Rice: You'll need a cup of uncooked white rice. Short grain rice will yield a thicker and creamier final dish.
  • Milks: This recipe needs three types of milk: whole milk, evaporated milk, and sweetened condensed milk.

Steps

  1. Bring the water and cinnamon stick to a boil, then simmer.
  2. Add the rice, bring to a boil, and simmer until the water is absorbed.
  3. Stir in the milk, evaporated milk, and sweetened condensed milk.
  4. Garnish with cinnamon for serving.
